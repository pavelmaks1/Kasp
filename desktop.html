What are your kids doing when they’re holding a smartphone or tablet?

Your child may be experiencing cyberbullying on the internet…

Because sometimes you simply don’t know what your child is doing online. Maybe everything is fine and they’re just watching movies or playing games. But maybe not, and right now someone is hounding and bullying your kid in an online chat. And you’ll never know, if you don’t investigate your kids’ online life.

Today’s children and teenagers are deeply immersed in the world of virtual communications, even deeper than adults. They take everything that happens to them online very seriously.

Bad, right?
So How Do You Protect Your Kid Online?
Here are three simple but useful TIPS:
  1. Educate children about safety online

    Unobtrusively discuss the importance of safety on the internet with your kids. Simple but vital things such as not sharing personal data with strangers.

  2. Limit time on devices

    Manage the amount of time your child spends gaming or browsing. Strike a balance between digital life and real life.

  3. Use a parental control app

    With Kaspersky Safe Kids it’s much easier to set up the required level of digital security for your child (blocks inappropriate content, unwanted communications, etc.).
